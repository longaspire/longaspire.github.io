Indoor Venue Keyword Dataset

published by RUC and AAU

Description

This is the synthetic indoor keyword data used in our paper Indoor Top-k Keyword-aware Routing Query published in ICDE'20. The keyword set covers the following five real-world shopping malls in Hong Kong.

  1. City Plaza. http://www.cityplaza.com/en/shopping.aspx/.
  2. Festival Walk. http://www.festivalwalk.com.hk/en/shopping/.
  3. Harbour City. http://www.harbourcity.com.hk/en/shop-dine/shop/.
  4. New Town Plaza. http://www.newtownplaza.com.hk/shopping/.
  5. Times Square. https://timessquare.com.hk/shopping?lang=en.
