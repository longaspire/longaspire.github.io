Personal Information

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow at Department of Computer Science, Aalborg University. Before joining Aalborg University, I was a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Ant Financial (Alipay) of Alibaba Group. I received my Ph.D. degree from Zhejiang University, China. My research interests include mobile/pervasive computing, vision-based localization, and indoor mobility data management/analysis. I have published 7 relevant papers at top outlets including TKDE, VLDB, ICDE, and UbiComp.

Apart from the issues related to computer science, I am a sports lover, particularly for soccer. I also enjoy traveling and spending time with my family and friends.
